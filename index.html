Fat Burning Support

Capsaicin, the active compound in cayenne, activates transient receptor potential vanilloid (TRPV1) channels which stimulate heat production in the body. Leptin Sensitivity

Studies show meaningful reductions in waist circumference with regular use over 8 to 10 weeks.

Apple Cider Vinegar
Appetite and Glucose Control

Acetic acid in apple cider vinegar slows gastric emptying, which reduces the speed at which carbohydrates enter the bloodstream and moderates post-meal insulin responses. Research suggests daily apple cider vinegar intake reduces fasting blood glucose and modestly reduces total calorie intake across the day. Its liquid form in this shot avoids the esophageal irritation associated with undiluted ACV.

Ashwagandha Root
Cortisol Reduction

Ashwagandha is an adaptogenic herb with well-documented cortisol-lowering effects. Clinical evidence indicates it reduces serum cortisol by an average of 27 percent in chronically stressed adults, which directly reduces stress-driven abdominal fat accumulation. It also supports thyroid function, which governs baseline metabolic rate in adults over 35.

B Vitamin Complex
Metabolic Cofactor

B vitamins including B6, B12, and niacin are essential cofactors in the metabolic pathways that convert carbohydrates, fats, and proteins into usable energy. Deficiency in any B vitamin slows these pathways and reduces the body's ability to efficiently metabolize stored fat. Garcinia Cambogia
Fat Synthesis Inhibitor

Hydroxycitric acid (HCA) from Garcinia cambogia inhibits the enzyme ATP citrate lyase, which the body uses to convert excess carbohydrates into fatty acids for storage.
